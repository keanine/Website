Details

Puzzle your way through various different rooms and chambers by using your reflective shield to reflect all sorts of dangerous paraphernalia! A top down game where you reflect bullets into buttons, and lasers into sensors to open doors and progress through the levels. If a singleplayer experience isn't your sort of thing, why not try out the arena. Here you can challenge your friends and foes into a match of Reflection. Use the bullets and/or lasers in your chosen map to take out the other player, and use mutators to change up the battle!

Reflection was made not long after the release of Unity 5 so the game needs an update and some love to get it working properly again. When the update is complete, new media will be added to this page to show off the game properly.
