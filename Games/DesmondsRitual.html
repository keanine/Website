Details

Desmond's Ritual is a puzzle game for PC and Android in which you try to summon the all powerful demon, Desmond! You must use the clues in order to complete combinations of ingredients, so that you can complete the ritual. This game was created during Global Games Jam 2016 in 48 hours.

This was the first time that I worked on a game with a team that consisted of more than two people, so it was definitely a learning experience for me. Our time management wasn't fantastic and most of us ended up getting very little sleep. As well as this, we learnt the importance of version control. With this project, we actually did not use it and struggled with making sure everyones work was implemented and up to date as a result.
