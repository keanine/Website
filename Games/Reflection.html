Details

Puzzle your way through various different rooms and chambers by using your reflective shield to reflect all sorts of dangerous paraphernalia! Reflect bullets into buttons, and lasers into sensors to open doors and progress through the levels. If a singleplayer experience isn't your sort of thing, why not try out the arena. Here you can challenge your friends and foes into a match of Reflection. Use the bullets and/or lasers in your chosen map to take out the other player, and use mutators to change up the battle!

Reflection was made in Unity 5, and is close to being complete. The base mechanics are tight and the reflection itself is accurate enough to be indistinguishable for players.
