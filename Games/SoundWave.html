Details

Guide your companion using commands through challenging puzzles using the soundwaves to anticipate obstacles.

This game was created for Global Games Jam 2017 in 48 hours, where the theme was "waves". Our team went through a few iterations of game ideas at first, but eventually we settled on a game in which you wave to your companion to make them follow you. It was only after development had started that we decided to add in the main mechanic of sound wave!

Music is a very important part of Sound-Wave. Obstacles only move on the beat, so you need to time your moves carefully as to avoid the drills and lasers! This isn't all though, as you still need to guide your friend by calling him and getting him to jump over the obstacles in his way. In one particular level at the end, you must do some difficult platforming in order to reach a button at the top, but there is a closed door in the way! In order to get past it, you need to tell your friend to stay on a button at the beginning. However there is a laser that fires over the button every 4 beats. This means that while you are platforming, you must time your command for your friend to jump as well!

Before the Games Jam even started we encountered a pretty big issue. Three of the six members of our team dropped out a few days before the event due to other commitments. This demoralised the remaining members of the team including myself, however we decided to push ahead anyway and see what we could do as a smaller group! I am very happy with the product we ended up with over those 48 hours, and our scope and time management turned out far better than last years attempt.
