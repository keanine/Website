Help the heroes travel through the Interdimensional Crossroads, fighting bad guys and gaining new abilities as they try to save the very essence of bacon itself! Rogue Squadron - Unity (WIP)
Visit locations from the Star Wars universe and fight battles from the movies you remember in this Unity Fan-Project! This will include the Death Star Attack, the Battle of Hoth, and the Battle of Endor! The project is currently incomplete, however the basic systems are in place.
MTG Life Counter
This Unity based application was designed for Android to help "Magic the Gathering" users to keep track of their life, posion and land. It is simple, but allows the user to change the background of each side of the app. The starting player is randomly decided, and the starting players "End Turn" button will be active.
