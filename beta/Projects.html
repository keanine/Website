About

In Vain is a Third Person Action Adventure through a dystopian city of freaks and frauds. Use your different combos and abilities to take out the wacky inhabitants of the city, and try and find your place in a world that has no rules. When you are close to death in this game, you can activate your second phase for a new lease of life, with a new moveset and gameplay style!
